CHINA TOWN STORE STEALS SHOW FOR CHEAP PRODUCTS IN UGANDA


September 3 rd, 2024
Clients seen entering China town store located in Lugogo on Tuesday

What you need to know

Products at China town are extremely cheap in that a tote bag that can be bought in down town at 40,000 drops from 40,000 to 20,000 in the store. Most of the Ugandans see this as an opportunity to get a hold of some items that were always hard to buy due to their extremely high prices from fellow native shops or supermarkets.
We didn't know that the social media influencers were advertising for us indirectly. We have been seeing people coming to the stores, they take videos of our products and end up sharing them yet we didn't even pay anyone to do that Says Mr. Daniel Akwanget, the marketing manager of the store.
